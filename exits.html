Affiliations are open! I would love to get connected with you and your site and give each other support. I'd like to make contacts with you if you have a site (or two) that relates to any one below:

  • If you have a Sakura x Syaoran-related site, whether if it's another shrine, a support clique, etc.
  • If you have a CCS-related site, be it another character shrine or a general news site.
  • If you have a CLAMP-related site, be it a general news site about CLAMP or a site/shrine of another series created by CLAMP.
  • If you have a kick-ass couple/pairing shrine from any series of any genre. What I mean by a kick-ass couple/pairing is if both of them have their own brand of kick-ass cool as individuals and work well together in a duo. For example, if the duo are both warriors or sorcerors or brainiacs, that kinda thing. (Like Sakura and Syaoran, who are both sorcerors in their own rights.)
  • If you have a character shrine/site to brave and heroic females (in their own way).
  • If you have a character shrine/site to brave and heroic males (in their own way).
  • If you have a site/shrine with a theme similar to CCS (sorcery, Asian sorcery, Western sorcery (especially British/English/Celtic sorcery), etc.)
